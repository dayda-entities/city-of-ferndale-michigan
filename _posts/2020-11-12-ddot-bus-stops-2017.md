Most current available data on bus stops in the city of Detroit, MI. Data reflects service as of April 22, 2017 and is subject to change as DDOT continues to improve service. All stops included in this dataset are operated by DDOT staff, including SE Michigan RTA's Reflex 498 Woodward.
