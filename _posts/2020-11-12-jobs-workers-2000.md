This dataset includes average commute time from the 2010-2014 American Community Survey. It also includes totals and densities for both Primary Jobs and Resident Workers from 2012 - 2014, It identifies totals and percentages of jobs that are held by residents of Detroit, Highland Park, or Hamtramck (DHPH), and totals and percentages of resident workers who work in DHPH.
